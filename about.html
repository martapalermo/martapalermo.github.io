MARTA PALERMO

Hi, I'm Marta!

I just recently moved to Seattle from Boston, but I hail all the way from Milan Italy, where I was born and raised. In Boston I attended Northeastern University, for my undergrad, where I got my BSc in Neuroscience. For the longest time I thought I wanted to go to medical school, so a lot of my past course work and professional work experience will certainly reflect that!

I spent a few years working at Massachusetts General Hospital as an MRI Research Technician/ Clinical Research Coordinator. After our numerous guest speakers talking about the overlap between the two fields of Neuroscience and Computer Science, my interest peaked and I decided to make the switch and get my Masters in CS. I am now in my second year of my MSCS at Northeastern and am more excited than ever to continue pursuing this exciting and ever evolving field!
